About App

The Alzheimer Detection Web App is a user-friendly and powerful tool that simplifies the process of training, predicting, and tracking experiments for Alzheimer's disease detection. With its intuitive interface and integrated MLflow functionality, the app offers a seamless experience for healthcare professionals and researchers. This app provides an easy-to-use platform for training machine learning models specific to Alzheimer's disease. Users can effortlessly upload and preprocess their data, configure and fine-tune models, and monitor the training process. The app's robust prediction capabilities allow users to obtain accurate and reliable results. To enhance the experimental workflow, the app seamlessly integrates with MLflow, a popular open-source platform for managing machine learning experiments. Users can track and compare multiple experiments, access detailed metrics and visualizations, and make data-driven decisions based on the experiment results. Whether you are a healthcare professional working on Alzheimer's research or a data scientist exploring the potential of machine learning, the Alzheimer Detection Web App is your go-to solution. Experience the ease of training and predicting with integrated MLflow tracking, and unlock new insights in Alzheimer's disease detection.
